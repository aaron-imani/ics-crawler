WICS Spring Quarter Week 6: Study Session

05/17/201905/17/2019admin

On Monday, May 6, WICS hosted a study session, where students could meet up with friends and club members to help each other study for midterms and work on projects. The members helped each other with homework and gave advice and support to everyone. It was a great night to study and de-stress with kind peers over dinner!
