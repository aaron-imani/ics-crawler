Spring 2022 Week 2: WICS x CTC Personal Portfolio Workshop

04/21/2022admin

On Monday, April 4th, 2022, of Week 2, WICS held a personal portfolio workshop in collaboration with CTC. WICS members and ICS students came together to learn about building personal portfolios specifically for SWE and UI/UX roles. The workshop covered various topics including what to include in personal portfolios, how to start building your first portfolio, and the resources available online. The workshop also showed designs of personal portfolios from other ICS students. Overall, members learned important skills on how to build a personal portfolio during this informational and engaging workshop.
