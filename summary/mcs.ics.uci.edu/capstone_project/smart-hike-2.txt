Smart Hike #2 



Project #:  2 




Members:Asrar Zaman, Saurabh Khandelwal, Siddhant Jain, Wajeeha Fathima






Description:Smart Hike helps hikers find nearby hikes and provides detailed information about each trail — like length and difficulty. Using advanced filters, users can search and select trails they are comfortable exploring. It also leverages past hikes and preferences to provide personalized recommendation, making it easier to set out on an adventure right away.
