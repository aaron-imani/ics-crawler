Spring 2022 Week 1: WICS First General Meeting

04/18/202204/21/2022admin

On Monday, March 28, 2022, of Week 1, WICS held our First General Meeting for the spring quarter. WICS members and members from the ICS community joined in to learn more about WICS, WICS Board and Committee, and upcoming WICS events! After, everyone enjoyed some pizza and time chatting with each other about the quickly beginning spring quarter.
