Winter 2022 Week 1: General Meeting + Paint Night Social

01/11/202201/31/2022admin

On Monday, January 3, 2021, of Week 1, WICS held the First General Meeting for the winter quarter. Around 50 students gathered on Zoom to learn more about WICS and WICS’ upcoming events, including, the Mock Technical Interview and Game Night! Attendees later competed in a light-hearted pop-quiz about WICS’ fun facts, and members got the chance to win gift cards. To cap off the night, WICS hosted a virtual paint night where 4-5 members collaborated on creating a painting and then shared the paintings with the group.
