WICS Fall Quarter Week 1: First General Meeting

10/23/2020admin

On Monday, October 5th, 2020, of Week 1, over 100 members of the ICS community at UCI came together to learn about what WICS has in store for the rest of the quarter. At the start of the meeting, members listened to popular “throwback” music and got the chance to mingle with each other and new members! General members also got put into breakout groups to play virtual board games and connect with WICS Board and Committee Members.
