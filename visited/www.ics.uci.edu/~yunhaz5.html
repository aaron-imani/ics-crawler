About

I'm a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UC Irvine, advised by Prof. Charless Fowlkes. Prior to joining UC Irvine, I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Alan Yuille at Johns Hopkins University. I have also spent a wonderful time working with Prof. Rene Vidal and Prof. Joshua Tenenbaum for a short period.

Research Interests
  • My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision and machine learning, especially deep learning.
  • My current research focuses include 1) 3D scene understanding, 2) domain adaptation, 3) generative models
