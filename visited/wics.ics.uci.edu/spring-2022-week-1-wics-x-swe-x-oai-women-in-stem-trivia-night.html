Spring 2022 Week 1: WICS x SWE x OAI Women in STEM Trivia Night

04/21/2022admin

On Tuesday, March 29, 2022, WICS, SWE, and OAI held a Trivia Night in celebration of Women’s History Month! There were questions on various female historical figures as well as trivia on female faculty members. The night was very educational and attendees were able to learn about important women in STEM figures that weren’t recorded in textbooks. Everyone had so much fun munching on pizza while answering the trivia questions! At the end, the winners were rewarded with gift cards and all members took a photo together.
