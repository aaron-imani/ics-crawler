Spring 2022 Week 4: Projects at Chipotle

05/01/202205/01/2022admin

On Monday April 18, 2022, WICS hosted the Projects at Chipotle event in partnership with Chipotle! WICS members joined together in person and connected with representatives from Chipotle through Zoom to dive deeper into the various projects Chipotle does, specifically Extras through the Chipotle app. There were representatives from the Program Management sector, Product Design sector, and Software Development sector, and each representative discussed their experiences and what they contribute to the team. Following the presentation by Chipotle, WICS members asked various questions, which were answered by the Chipotle representatives. Overall, the Projects at Chipotle session was an insightful look into how Chipotle takes on projects and executes them.
