WICS Winter Quarter Week 7: WICS Round 1 Social

02/23/201902/28/2019admin

On February 20th, WICS members went out to Santa Ana for a social break at Round 1 bowling! There were roughly 20 people who joined WICS for this event. We began the social by eating dinner at the food court in Main Place Mall before heading over to Round 1 bowling. Once getting into the arcade section, we all cheered on our friends as they won game after game and received many tickets to use towards a future prize! We then booked a karaoke room and sang our favorite hits from today all the way back to the early 2000s. We ended the night with $1 ice cream and cute pictures to take the amazing memories home! The WICS members learned new traits about each other as we bonded over similar music tastes and food!
