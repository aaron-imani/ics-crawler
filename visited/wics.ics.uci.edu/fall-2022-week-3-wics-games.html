Fall 2022 Week 3: WICS Games

10/12/2022admin

On Monday, October 10, 2022, over 100 people gathered in Donald Bren Hall for WICS Games 2022! This annual event brings company representatives and eager ICS students together for a night of riddles, coding questions, networking, and fun! The group was split up into 12 teams, who competed to solve their questions and earn points. Each team worked with their company representative to create a team name and chant, which they had to perform for everyone. Pasta, breadsticks, and salad fueled the participants as they solved the questions, with the help of their company representative and a WICS buddy. In the end, three teams came out on top and were awarded prizes. Shout-out to the representatives from Crowdstrike, Meta, CoStar Group, Slalom, and Northrop Grumman for volunteering their time to make WICS Games 2022 a success!
