Spring 2022 Week 7: Crowdstrike Office Hours

05/15/2022admin

On Monday, May 9th, 2022, members of WICS gathered in front of the big screen to listen to representatives from Crowdstrike talk about their work in cybersecurity. Although the representatives told their stories through Zoom, the members got to come together in person to listen, ask questions, and enjoy some free pizza! WICS heard from representatives of all different backgrounds and job titles, some even joining Crowdstrike without the initial intention to work in this industry. Everyone got to learn a lot about what they do at Crowdstrike, what their internship program is like, and the best parts of their jobs.
