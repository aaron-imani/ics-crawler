Facts and figures
BREN SCHOOL FACTS
  • Only computing-focused school in UC system
  • Founded in 1968 as Department of Information and Computer Science
  • Established in 2002 as an independent school, the Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences (ICS)
RANKINGS
  • US News & World Report (2022)
    #14 Computer Science graduate program and #12 Computer Science undergraduate program among public universities
     #7 Software Engineering undergraduate program
    #18 Statistics graduate program among public universities
  • Microsoft Academic Search website ranking of authors and organizations by citations (as of March 11, 2014)
    UC Irvine 20th among U.S. universities in computer science. Some top subareas:
    - Human-computer interaction: 4th
    - Software engineering: 9th
    - Databases: 8th

  • 11 Fellows of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE): Pierre Baldi, Michael Carey, Nikil Dutt, Michael Franz, Michael Goodrich, Ramesh Jain, Crista Lopes, Sharad Mehrotra, Alexandru Nicolau, Marios Papaefthymiou, Gene Tsudik
