Winter 2022 Week 7: WICSentine

02/27/202202/27/2022admin

On Monday, February 14th, 2022, of Week 7, WICS gathered together at WICSentine, an event to celebrate Valentine day! After the welcome, a bouquet making session was held. Everyone got a chance to make their own bouquet out of wires and papers and bring it home too! To add a touch of thrill, members also played 3 rounds of musical chair during the bouquet making session. Closing off the night, members show appreciation for others by writing them a heartwarming letter. At the end of the event, everyone got together and took a picture with the bouquet that they made.
