The one-day symposium at the University of California, Irvine, brings researchers, leaders in the industry, and technical practitioners to Southern California together to discuss trends in the field of software engineering, showcase current research, and formulate visions on strategic future research and technological directions.

Professor David Redmiles will give a talk on “Human Aspects of Software Engineering” during the faculty presentation 1. Besides, two PhD. student Zhendong and Yang will showcase their research during the open house poster presentation. Come by and chat with us!
