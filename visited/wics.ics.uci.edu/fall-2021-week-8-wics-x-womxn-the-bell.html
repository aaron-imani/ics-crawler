Fall 2021 Week 8: WICS x Womxn @ The Bell

11/29/202111/29/2021admin

This Wednesday, November 17, 2021, of Week 8, WICS held a Taco Bell Info Session via Zoom to learn more about the company and internship opportunities for Summer 2022. After a brief introduction from the Taco Bell panelists, the members were encouraged to ask questions to them through sli.do. Throughout the session, the panelists shared many valuable insights such as their career development journeys, advice to interns/new grads, and the working environment at the company. The session ended well with uplifting energies from the Taco Bell panelists.
