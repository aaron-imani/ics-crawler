2nd Annual
ICS Project Expo

June 1, 2022 • 3:30-6:30 p.m.
UCI's Interdisciplinary Science and Engineering Building

Please join us on Wednesday, June 1, 2022, for the 2nd Annual ICS Project Expo presented by the undergraduate capstone programs from Informatics, Computer Science, and Data Science. There will also be some showcase projects from the ICS Honors Program and Butterworth Design Competition. This year's event will be back in person at the new Interdisciplinary Science and Engineering Building on campus.

  • Interact with ICS students showcasing their capstone projects
  • Learn about capstone programs in ICS
  • Enjoy refreshments during poster session
  • Mingle with industry experts
  • Connect with the ICS Alumni Chapter
  • Experience the state-of-the-art ISEB facility
