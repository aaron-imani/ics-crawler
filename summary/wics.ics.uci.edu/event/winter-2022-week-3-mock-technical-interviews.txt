WICS invites you to 2022 Mock Technical Interviews! Sign up for a 30 minute Software Engineering, UI/UX or Program Management interview with a company representative from Amazon, Chipotle, Cisco, GitHub, Intel, MathWorks, Veeva or Vimeo! This is a great opportunity to brush up on your technical interviewing skills and to get real-time feedback from industry professionals. Interview slots are first come first serve, so sign up as soon as you can. A Zoom link will be emailed out to those who RSVP.
